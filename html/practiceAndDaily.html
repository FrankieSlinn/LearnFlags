FLAGL - The Daily Flag Quiz


Guess the Flag

Each day every FLAGL Player will see the same five flags. The goal of course is to get as many flags right as you can. All you need to do is to start typing in the box under the flag to get a list of countries and territories. Click one of the country or territory buttons to make your choice.

At the end of the flag quiz you will see what percentage of flags you got right. The statistics section on the top right shows your average score per game so you can see if you're improving. Share and compare your score with friends by selecting the "Share Results" button in the statistics section.
They might be wowed by your flag guessing ability. Or maybe not. There's only one way of telling.

Does this format look familiar? The game is inspired by the brilliant game Worlde created by the talented Josh Wardle.
