How the Game Works

Guess the Flag.
As you start typing a list of possible countries will appear. The more letters you type, the fewer the options become. Click a box to select your choice.
Each game has five guesses. At the end of the game you will see what percentage you got right. The statistics section on the right shows the average score per game.
The statistics are shareable.
